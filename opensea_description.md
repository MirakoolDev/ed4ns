**Surf: Forgot the Ground** is the debut open edition from *ed4ns* — an on-chain elimination arena where every token competes for the prize pool.

---

### How it works

- **Mint** a token during the open edition window
- Once minting closes, the **arena begins**
- Every round, **half the remaining tokens are eliminated** using on-chain randomness derived from block hashes — no oracles, no human intervention
- Rounds continue until **4 tokens remain**
- 10mins cooldown period** after each elimination round before the next one can be triggered.
- The final holders **split the entire ETH prize pool equally**, claimable directly from the contract

---

### Rules

- Token status follows the token, not the wallet. Buy or sell freely.
- Anyone can trigger the next cut once the cooldown expires.
